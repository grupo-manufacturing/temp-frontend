Responza Inbox: WhatsApp Customer Messaging

Connect a WhatsApp Business account to send support updates and receive customer replies in one workspace.

Status: Not connected

Why we request these permissions

This application asks for only the access required to provide customer messaging features.

  • whatsapp_business_management: connect your WhatsApp Business Account and phone sender.
  • whatsapp_business_messaging: send approved customer messages and receive customer replies.

You can disconnect the account in Meta Business settings at any time.

What happens after you click connect

1) Authenticate with MetaSign in and approve requested permissions for your business account.
2) Account is linkedResponza links your WhatsApp Business Account and sender number.
3) Start messagingSend outbound updates and monitor incoming customer replies in the inbox below.
