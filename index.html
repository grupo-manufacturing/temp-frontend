Instagram

  • instagram_business_basic

    Identify and verify your Instagram Business account (user id, username) after login so we can show connection status and route conversations to your inbox.

  • instagram_business_manage_messages

    Receive webhook notifications for inbound DMs, load thread history via the Messaging API where available, and send replies you compose in this inbox.

WhatsApp

  • whatsapp_business_management

    Used during Embedded Signup to onboard your WhatsApp Business Account (WABA) and phone number id, link them to this app, and persist tokens needed for webhook delivery.

  • whatsapp_business_messaging

    Exchange messages with opted-in users: receive inbound webhooks for this tenant, render threads in the inbox, and send outbound text when you tap Send.
